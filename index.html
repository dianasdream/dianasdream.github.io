Welcome to Diana's Dream

East Brunswick, New Jersey

January 16, 2026 Audrey Weiner

Diana's Dream is A Place for All - we are a non-profit based in East Brunswick, NJ dedicated to empowering young adults of all abilities. By partnering with schools through SLE 18-21 programs and creating opportunities for special needs adults over 21, we provide pathways to meaningful employment, personal growth, social connection, and the skills to thrive. Our vision is a community where every individual can live life to the fullest. We welcome and embrace community support on all levels to help make this dream a reality.
